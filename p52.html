’Please do not feed the animals…’

Please do not the ostriches
sandwiches

feed

or the polar bears
eclairs.

Do not the wombats
kumquats

offer

or the rattle-snakes
fruit-cakes.

Remember that piranhas
are not bananas

allowed

or partridges
sausages.

Never a stork
with things on a fork

approach

or the bustard
with a plate of custard.

No leopard
anything peppered

likes

and meercats
KitKats.

disdain

Remember that grapes
apes

upset

and meringues
the same for orangutans.

do

Most importantly –
do not the cheetah

feed

your teacher.
